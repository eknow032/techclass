This is one of the exciting parts: time to pick your theme. The sky is the limit here, although do make sure that you're not infringing on copywrite (go with Magic rather than Harry Potter, for example). As with everything you do, be sure to engage your girls in choosing this theme. Have them discuss, brainstorm, vote...Whatever works for you!

Your theme is a great time to link what you're doing to some program work and I bet you can do this without the girls even knowing, if that's how you roll!
